The Villas

Two villas.
Two stories.

Side by side on Sifa's shoreline, Casa del Mar and Casa del Sol share the same private beach and pool. Each has its own distinct character. Choose the one that speaks to you.

🌊 Casa del Mar
Casa del Mar
House of the Sea

Casa del Mar faces the water head-on. Built for those who want to wake up with the sea already in view. The kind of morning where you open the doors and the sound of waves fills the room before your coffee does. Cool blues, natural stone, and a terrace that feels like it's floating above the beach. This villa is for the ones who came for the ocean.

2 bedrooms Sea-facing terrace 4 guests max En-suite bathrooms Full kitchen

Casa del Sol
House of the Sun

Casa del Sol is bathed in afternoon light. Warm tones, open-air living, and a south-facing terrace that catches the sun from noon until it drops into the sea. The pool feels different here: golden hour turns the water amber. This villa is for the ones who stay outside until dark. For gatherings around the BBQ, long evenings, and the kind of sunset that makes you want to come back.

2 bedrooms Sun terrace 4 guests max En-suite bathrooms Full kitchen

Getting Here

Sifa, Oman

📍Map — Sifa, Oman

Sifa sits on the Al Batinah South coast, roughly 45 minutes from Muscat along the scenic coastal highway. The drive passes through dramatic cliffs and hidden coves. It's part of the experience.

Both villas are accessible by car. Full address and GPS coordinates are included with your booking confirmation.
